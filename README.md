<p align="center">

The **Student Congress on Smart & Sustainable Technologies (S3T 2026)** is a multidisciplinary academic event hosted at  
<b>Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Ludhiana</b>.

The congress provides a platform for **undergraduate students, postgraduate students, and research scholars** to present innovative ideas, research findings, and engineering solutions addressing real-world challenges.

Aligned with the vision of **Viksit Bharat @ 2047**, S3T 2026 aims to foster **innovation, sustainability, and interdisciplinary collaboration** among the next generation of engineers.

</p>

## Conference Tracks

S3T 2026 invites submissions across the following engineering disciplines:

- **Civil Engineering** — Sustainable Infrastructure & Resilient Built Environment  
- **Electrical Engineering** — Smart Energy Systems & Electrification  
- **Electronics & Communication Engineering** — AI, IoT & Future Electronics  
- **Mechanical & Production Engineering** — Smart Manufacturing & Sustainable Systems

## Paper Submission

Authors are invited to submit **4–5 page technical papers** related to the conference themes.

Accepted submissions may include:

- Review Papers  
- Experimental Studies  
- Case Studies  
- Technology Development Papers  
- Analytical or Conceptual Studies  

All accepted and presented papers will be published in the **S3T 2026 Conference Proceedings (ISBN).**
